Biography

I am a biostatistician at Children's Mercy Research Institute in Kansas City, Missouri. My research interests include seamless oncology clinical trial methodology and statistical methods for spatial protein imaging data.

Research

  • Trivariate co-localization via Delaunay triangle area + Horvitz–Thompson Monte Carlo estimator.
  • Multiplex imaging pipeline in QuPath/ImageJ to reduce misclassification and support TLS identification.
